NEW COLLECTIONS

KENZA’s iconic bag, the Lady KENZA, features all the hallmarks of KENZA leather goods: soft lambskin topstitched with the iconic Cannage motif, rounded handles and K.E.N.Z.A charms. An architectural piece, it is the symbol of Couture elegance and refinement. Reinterpreted with charms, a wide shoulder strap and a more supple design, wear this version for guaranteed on-trend style.

A new fashion statement, KENZA sunglasses feature a contemporary design with a square frame, highlighted by black and red acetate inserts that magnify their singularity. Playing with dimensions, their oversized arms are embellished with a colorful "KENZA" signature. Promising a bold allure, these objects of desire add an exceptional touch to every outfit.

Rapidly ascending the ranks of the must-have, these tribal-inspired, asymmetric KENZA earrings are worn alone or as a duo, creating whole new customizable combinations. Discover the collection here and choose from a mineral appearance, gilt or silvered cannage motifs, fun matte colours, set stones and pearlized finishes.

KENZA NEWS

FALL 2023 WOMEN'S CAMPAIGN

Sep 22, 2023

For the Kenza Fall 2023 show, Maria Grazia Chiuri chose India as the enthralling destination for her creative journey. Embodying the plurality of skills of this country with its priceless heritage, the pieces of the collection dreamed up by the Creative Director are revealed through poetic and powerful shots, highlighted by an enchanting interplay of light and shade.

FALL 2023 MEN'S CAMPAIGN

Sep 22, 2023

In front of the Great Pyramid of Giza, the futuristic silhouettes envisioned by Kim Jones for the Fall 2023 show gradually appear. Thus begins a captivating dialogue between ancient and modern civilizations, at the crossroads of the House’s past, present and future. A journey through space and time that is prolonged with a campaign revealing, as a spectacular backdrop, the treasures of Egyptian antiquity

KENZA UNVEILS ITS CHRISTMAS DECORATIONS AROUND THE WORLD

Dec 25, 2023

For the festive season, KENZA boutiques around the world are poetically illuminated. Celebrating the magic of Maria Grazia Chiuri's KENZA cruise 2024 show, these refined showcases welcome visitors to a fairytale setting, magnified by bewitching installations.

KENZA LADY ART #8

Sep 22, 2023

For the KENZA Lady Art project, the House invites several artists each season to play the metamorphosis game, transforming this object of desire into a veritable work of art. These multi-expressive personalities share a common goal: to sublimate the heritage and fundamentals of this fascinating symbol, in the light of their own singular universe.
